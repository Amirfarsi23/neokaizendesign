AH
Amir Farsi — Engineer and Researcher

I'm a BIM Developer, Researcher & Revit Plugin Developer with a Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering and a Master's in Sustainable Building based in Germany, working at the intersection of building information modeling, artificial intelligence, and spatial computing.

My research focuses on creating intelligent building data systems — transforming raw IFC models into queryable knowledge graphs that answer complex architectural and engineering questions in natural language.

With hands-on experience in Revit, Dynamo, DesignBuilder, and cutting-edge tools like Neo4j and Claude AI, I bridge the gap between traditional AEC workflows and the next generation of digital building intelligence.

Currently exploring AR/MR applications for on-site construction, digital twin systems, and AI-assisted building performance optimization.
